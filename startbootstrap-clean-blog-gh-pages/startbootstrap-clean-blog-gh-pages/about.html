About Me

This is who I am

Hey there! I'm Olivia Little, a California girl born and raised in the sunny city of Bakersfield. Design, learning, and creating connections that last a lifetime are what I'm all about. Growing up in a family-oriented environment, I developed a deep appreciation for the importance of lasting relationships. That's why I'm thrilled to announce that I'm marrying the man of my dreams. I know, it sounds like a fairytale, but it's true! We're both hopeless romantics and can't wait to start our journey together, building a loving family that cherishes adventure and making a positive impact on the world.

Now, let's talk about my passion for design and pretty things. Pink is my ultimate obsession! From the softest pastel hues to vibrant magentas, you'll find touches of pink in every aspect of my life. It's like my personal signature color. I believe that incorporating beauty and elegance into every event is crucial. As an aspiring event planner, I aim to create unforgettable experiences, and what better way to do that than with stunning floral arrangements? Peonies, in particular, hold a special place in my heart. Their delicate petals and captivating fragrance make them an absolute must-have in any event I organize.

In addition to my love for design, I find solace and creativity in the kitchen. Cooking and baking have always been my escape, where I can let my imagination run wild. There's something incredibly satisfying about turning a bunch of ingredients into a mouthwatering masterpiece. As an event planner, I bring this culinary passion to the table by infusing every occasion with delectable treats and beautifully crafted dishes. Whether it's a wedding, a birthday celebration, or a corporate event, I believe that good food brings people together and creates lasting memories.

So, if you're searching for an event planner who not only understands the significance of meaningful connections but also adds a touch of pink and culinary delights to your special day, you've come to the right place. Let's embark on this exciting journey together, where love, design, and delectable treats converge to create a truly unforgettable experience.
